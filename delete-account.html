KunoTheca Account Deletion

If you want to request deletion of your KunoTheca account or associated personal data, please send an email to contact@kunotheca.com using the email address linked to your account.

Please include:

  • Your account email address.
  • A clear request to delete your account and related data.
  • Any details needed to help identify your account, if relevant.

We may retain limited information when required for legal, security, or billing record purposes.
