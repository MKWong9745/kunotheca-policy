KunoTheca Support

Welcome to the official support page for KunoTheca, a language-learning app for creating personal dictionaries, vocabulary Cubes, and custom study content.

Contact support

For technical support, account assistance, or purchase questions, email us at contact@kunotheca.com.

Sign-in and password reset

You can sign in using a supported account method shown in the app. If you use email and password and cannot sign in, select the password reset option on the sign-in screen and check your inbox and spam folder for the reset email.

Dictionaries and Cubes

Dictionaries organize your learning collections. Each dictionary can contain vocabulary Cubes with text, images, and custom properties. If content does not appear after saving, confirm that you have a stable internet connection, then close and reopen the relevant list.

AI-assisted features and tokens

AI-assisted features run only when you choose to use them and may require tokens. Generated results can vary, so review suggested content before saving it. If a request fails, check your connection and token balance before trying again.

Subscriptions and purchases

Purchases are processed by the App Store. If an active subscription or purchase is not recognized, make sure you are signed in with the Apple Account used for the purchase, then use the Restore Purchases option in the app. You will not be charged again for restoring an eligible purchase.

Camera and photo library access

Camera and photo library access is optional and requested only when you choose to add an image. If access was previously denied, open the iOS Settings app, find KunoTheca, and enable the required permission.

Reporting a technical problem

To help us investigate quickly, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The KunoTheca app version.
  • A short description of what happened.
  • The steps that caused the problem.
  • A screenshot, if available and appropriate.

Do not include passwords, payment card details, or other sensitive information in your support message.
