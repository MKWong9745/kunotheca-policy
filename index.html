KunoTheca Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026
App name: KunoTheca (displayed in app as Kuno)

This Privacy Policy explains what data KunoTheca collects, how it is used, and what choices users have when using the app.

Summary: KunoTheca collects account information and app content needed to provide sign-in, syncing, learning features, purchases, and optional AI features. The app also requests camera access when you choose to capture an image.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information such as email address and authentication data.
  • Profile and settings information you provide inside the app.
  • Learning content you create, such as dictionaries, cubes, labels, and notes.
  • Images you choose to upload or capture for image-related features.
  • Purchase and subscription status required to unlock paid features.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To save and sync your app data across sessions.
  • To process purchases, subscriptions, and entitlements.
  • To provide AI-assisted features when you actively use them.
  • To improve product reliability, security, and support.

3. Camera and media access

KunoTheca requests camera access only for features where you choose to take a photo inside the app. If you do not grant camera permission, you can still use other parts of the app.

4. Third-party services

KunoTheca may use trusted third-party providers such as Firebase / Google Cloud for backend services, Google Sign-In for authentication, RevenueCat / Google Play for purchases, and AI providers for optional AI features.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers only as needed to operate the app and its features.

6. Data retention and deletion

User data is retained only as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and support account-related requests.

To request deletion of your account or data, please visit the account deletion page.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, account issues, or deletion requests, contact: contact@kunotheca.com

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
